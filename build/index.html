Nora's Website

Being me is kind of like reading a sequel to the Voynich Manuscript, and the entire time there is a crushing feeling of agnosthesia references to books I've never read, and a lot of meandering about midding. There is a trend towards altschmerz somewhere in the middle, and a longing for flashovers that may never come. The liberosis is palpable. With this last chapter of my life closing there is a focus on adronitis, and a desperation towards creating a new rigor samsa knowing that this will only exacerbate the disconnected sonder sonder I am prone to. I do what I can to submit myself to the mortifying ordeal, and yet whenever I have I've only felt mortified and never known.
